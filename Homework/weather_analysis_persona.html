Weather Purpose Statment

The site purpose is to keep travelers aware of current and future weather conditions.
wether there is sunny weather, cloudy weather or snowy weather, people need to have the information at their fingertips on 3 diferent devices.
Some people may have all 3 devices. Others may only have a laptop.

The internet connection depends on the financial state of the family.
they use the web for 10-20 minutes studying the weather forecast.
